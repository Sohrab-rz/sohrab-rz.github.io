About

Sohrab is a researcher specializing in the control of complex dynamical systems, with expertise in data-driven and AI methods for system analysis and control.

Earlier in his research, he explored non-parametric control (NPC) methods, data-driven structural analysis of multivariable systems, and data-driven control allocation for over-actuated systems.

He is particularly interested in data-driven approaches that bridge theory and practice, aiming to advance next-generation methodologies for modeling and control of complex systems.
